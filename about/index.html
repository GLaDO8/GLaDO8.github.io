About

Shreyas Gupta is a UX researcher and iOS developer studying at IIIT Bangalore, India. His research interests are technology design for sustainability and Digital Wellbeing.

Currently he is working with Dr. Bidisha Chaudhuri to understand how screen time features and other techniques which enforce non-use of technology, affect family dynamics in indian nuclear families. He has previously worked with GE Healthcare as a data science intern. to You can find his resume here.

In his free time, you'll find him experimenting with different user interfaces, rendering weird products on his potato MacBook Pro or rewatching clips from The Office for the millionth time.
